About

Independent energy & carbon expertise

EnergyDesk Australia gives businesses clear, independent guidance on energy, fuel and carbon, grounded in real market data and deep sector experience. Built for Australian business, and accessible to households too.

Why EnergyDesk exists

Australian businesses face a tangle of rising energy costs, volatile fuel prices and tightening carbon obligations, often with no independent place to turn. Energy brokers work on commission. Carbon software is expensive and built for big corporates. Real market data sits behind paywalls.

EnergyDesk sits in that gap: practical tools, plain-language analysis and tailored reports that put genuinely useful information within reach, without the sales agenda.

The experience behind it

EnergyDesk is built on extensive hands-on experience across the Australian energy and fuels sector. That means a real, practical understanding of how energy and fuel markets actually work, how businesses are billed, and where the genuine opportunities to cut cost and carbon sit.

That experience spans electricity and gas markets, LPG and liquid fuels, and the emerging world of bioenergy and carbon accounting, the exact areas where businesses most need clear guidance today.

What we stand for

Principles that guide every report

Independent & transparent

Our paid analysis is never influenced by commercial relationships. We earn referral fees from some partners, but always disclosed, never hidden. And we don't supply or sell business equipment, so our procurement guidance is exactly that: guidance.

Grounded in data

Every recommendation is built on real market figures, $/GJ comparisons and current emission factors, not opinion or guesswork.

Plain language

Energy and carbon are complicated enough. We explain things clearly, so you can make confident, defensible decisions.
